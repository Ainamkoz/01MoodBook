Perfect Summer Fruit Salad

General Infos

25 mins
cook:
5 mins

3 hrs

10
Yield:
10 servings


Ingredients (for 4 servings)
1/4 cup and 1 teaspoon fresh orange juice
2 tablespoons and 1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons and 1/2 teaspoon packed brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon grated orange zest
1/4 teaspoon grated lemon zest
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup and 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons cubed fresh pineapple
3/4 cup and 2 tablespoons strawberries, hulled and sliced
1-1/4 kiwi fruit, peeled and sliced
1-1/4 bananas, sliced
3/4 oranges, peeled and sectioned
1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon seedless grapes
3/4 cup and 2 teaspoons blueberries 


Preparation
Step 1
Bring orange juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, orange zest, and lemon zest to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat, and stir in vanilla extract. Set aside to cool.

Step 2
Layer the fruit in a large, clear glass bowl in this order: pineapple, strawberries, kiwi fruit, bananas, oranges, grapes, and blueberries. Pour the cooled sauce over the fruit. Cover and refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours before serving.
